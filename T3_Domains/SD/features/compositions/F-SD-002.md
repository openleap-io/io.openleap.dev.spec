## SS0. Identity

### 0.1 Purpose
This composition node groups all capabilities for planning outbound deliveries, executing shipments, and capturing proof of delivery. A logistics coordinator uses these features to translate confirmed sales orders into physical shipments and confirm customer receipt.

## SS1. Children & Variability Structure

### 1.1 Group Logic
**Group type:** mixed

**Business rationale:** Delivery Planning and Shipment Execution are mandatory because any physical fulfilment operation requires creating a delivery plan and then executing the shipment. Proof of Delivery is optional because some business models (e.g., digital goods or dropship) do not require explicit POD capture; it is enabled for organizations needing signed confirmation or carrier-confirmation workflows.

### 1.2 Sub-Groups

| Sub-group | Children | Group type | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Core fulfilment | `002-01`, `002-02` | mandatory | Minimum viable outbound logistics |
| Confirmation | `002-03` | optional | Required only where POD confirmation is a contractual or regulatory obligation |

### 1.3 Rationale for Tree Position
Shipping & Delivery depends on Order Management (F-SD-001) for its input — confirmed sales orders. It feeds Billing & Invoicing (F-SD-003) via goods issue events. It maps to the sd.dlv and sd.shp bounded contexts.

## SS2. Constraints

### 2.1 Intra-Node Constraints
| If | Then | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| `F-SD-002-02` is included | `F-SD-002-01` must be included | Shipment execution operates on deliveries created during planning; execution without planning is undefined |
| `F-SD-002-03` is included | `F-SD-002-02` must be included | Proof of delivery is recorded against a dispatched shipment; a shipment must exist before POD can be captured |

### 2.2 Cross-Node Constraints
| If | Then | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| `F-SD-002` is included | `F-SD-001` must be included | Deliveries are derived from confirmed sales orders; Order Management is a prerequisite |

## SS3. Quality & Testing Guidance
All leaf features MUST be independently testable. Integration tests MUST verify the full chain: delivery created from order in F-SD-002-01 → shipment dispatched in F-SD-002-02 → goods issue event triggers billing eligibility in F-SD-003-01. POD tests in F-SD-002-03 MUST verify that the `sd.dlv.delivery.pod-recorded` event is published.
