## SS1. Children & Variability Structure

### 1.1 Group Logic

**Group type:** mixed

**Business rationale for this structure:**

Product Backlog, Sprint Planning, and Kanban Board are mandatory within this composition because they represent the minimum agile toolset — a backlog to order work, sprints to time-box delivery, and a board to visualize flow. Burndown Chart, Velocity Tracking, and Custom Boards are optional enhancements that add agile metrics and customization but are not strictly required for basic agile workflow.

### 1.2 Mandatory Children

The following children are mandatory — they MUST be included whenever this composition node is selected:

- **F-PS-002-01** (Product Backlog): Required because it is part of the minimum viable agile & boards capability.
- **F-PS-002-02** (Sprint Planning & Management): Required because it is part of the minimum viable agile & boards capability.
- **F-PS-002-03** (Kanban Board): Required because it is part of the minimum viable agile & boards capability.

### 1.3 Optional Children

The following children are optional — they MAY be included based on product configuration:

- **F-PS-002-04** (Burndown Chart): Optional enhancement that adds value but is not required for basic agile & boards functionality.
- **F-PS-002-05** (Velocity Tracking): Optional enhancement that adds value but is not required for basic agile & boards functionality.
- **F-PS-002-06** (Custom Boards (Assignee/Version)): Optional enhancement that adds value but is not required for basic agile & boards functionality.

## SS3. Rationale & Decisions

### Why This Composition Node Exists

The Agile & Boards composition groups features that share the `ps-agl-svc` backend domain. These features collectively implement the agile & boards capability area of the PS suite. Grouping them under a single composition node allows:

1. **Product selection:** A product can include or exclude the entire agile & boards capability as a unit
2. **Mandatory minimum:** Within the composition, mandatory children ensure a viable minimum
3. **Optional extensions:** Optional children allow tailoring to organizational maturity

### Alternatives Considered

1. **Flat feature list (no composition):** Rejected because it would lose the ability to include/exclude the capability as a group and would make mandatory/optional relationships harder to express in UVL.
2. **Deeper nesting (sub-compositions):** Rejected because the current 6 children are manageable as a flat list under one composition. If the feature count grows beyond 12-15, re-evaluate.
